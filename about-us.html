Our History

Florist Chittagong: Blooming Since 2024, Where Flowers Paint Moments of Beauty.

Established in 2024, Florist has flourished into Chittagong's cherished haven for floral elegance and heartfelt connections. From our humble beginnings rooted in a passion for flowers, we've grown to become a beacon of creativity and quality in the community. Over the years, our commitment to crafting exquisite floral arrangements has brought smiles, celebrated milestones, and touched hearts across generations. At Florist, we continue to weave the beauty of nature into every bouquet, creating moments of lasting beauty and joy for our valued customers.
